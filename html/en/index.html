A solution provided by Newton Investment Partners.

Our mission is: “To realize the potential and maximize the value of Japanese companies by encouraging and supporting companies with management teams that aspire to transform their businesses through constructive dialogue based on relationships of mutual trust. We provide advisory and execution support, improving the market expectations of companies’ sustainable growth shaping their strategies and implementing action plans.

1.
Increase the number of companies on the Tokyo Stock Exchange that achieve high share price valuations by building sustainable growth expectations for higher multiples
During our investment period, we help companies transform into businesses that raise expectations for the future in order to increase the number of companies that can achieve higher valuations in a sustainable manner.
2.
Not only transforming our portfolio companies, but also positively impacting their related industries and the Japanese economy as a whole
By transforming our portfolio companies, we will make a positive impact on related industries, leading to higher growth for the Japanese economy.
3.
Fostering a virtuous economic cycle for the benefit of our environment and society
We will create a virtuous economic cycle and a sustainable economy for the benefit of the environment and our society by encouraging our portfolio companies to engage in activities that contribute to society and sustainability. Through the growth of our portfolio companies, profits will be distributed to both employees and SME business partners throughout the value chain, and we will look beyond institutional investors to also benefit individuals and stimulate further investment into the economy.

Through minority investments into companies listed on the Tokyo Stock Exchange, supports companies with management teams that aspire to transform their businesses by helping them to create expectations of long-term and sustainable growth for their stakeholders.
  • We invest in leading companies listed on the Prime Market of the Tokyo Stock Exchange
  • We build trust with management teams that have the will to transform their companies, and we will provide not only strategic advice but also support for the execution of strategic initiatives.
  • We enable management teams to take risks and implement bold strategic initiatives that fundamentally and sustainably improve their company’s growth profile and create additional value for shareholders. We do not recommend actions like share buybacks that will only improve a company’s share price in the short term.
We are founded by an investment professional from a leading global private equity firm who has a strong track record of supporting the successful transformation of a number of Japanese companies. Together with operating executives and senior advisors hailing from private equity firms, consulting firms, and investment banks who have experience of driving changes as senior executives at large corporations and financial institutions, we intend to leverage our diverse knowledge base and broad network to provide high value-added solutions to portfolio companies.
